About The Philly Animal Rights Alliance

The Philly Animal Rights Alliance (PARA) is a group for local animal rights activists across a variety of philosophies, organizations, and tactics to come together and network, share information and resources, increase cooperation, and collaborate on large-scale projects with the shared goal of creaing a sustainable movement for animal liberation in Phialdelphia.

We will be determining our shared values and goals at the first meeting. Take the pre-meeting survey to help us check the pulse of the community and share your opinion!

PARA is currently supported by five different national and local animal rights groups, as well as independent activists.
