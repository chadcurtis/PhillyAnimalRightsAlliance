About PARA

The Philly Animal Rights Alliance is a group for local animal rights activists across a variety of philosophies, organizations, and tactics to come to network, share information and resources, increase cooperation, and collaborate on large-scale projects

We will be determining out shared values and goals at our first meeting. Take the pre-meeting survey to help us check the pulse of the community and share your opinion!
