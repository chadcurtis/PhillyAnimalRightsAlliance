About The Philly Animal Rights Alliance

The Philly Animal Rights Alliance (PARA) is a group for local animal rights activists across a variety of philosophies, organizations, and tactics to come together and network, share information and resources, increase cooperation, and collaborate on large-scale projects with the shared goal of creaing a sustainable movement for animal liberation in Philadelphia.
